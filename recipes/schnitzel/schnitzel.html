Schnitzel

Schnitzel is a thin slice of veal or other light meat, coated in breadcrumbs and fried.

It is a famous and popular dish in Germany and Austria. Especially the viennese Schnitzel variant.

Must try!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Soak the meat in the eggs.
  2. Rotate meat in breadcrumbs
  3. Fry
  4. AServe with potatoes.
